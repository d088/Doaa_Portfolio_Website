Spotify Top Songs 2023 Analysis

Exploring music trends through data storytelling and Power BI

Project Overview

In 2023, Spotify dominated global streaming, and its top songs reflect evolving listener preferences. Using the Top Spotify Songs 2023 dataset, I explored key metrics like **popularity, danceability, energy, and acousticness** to understand what makes a hit song resonate with audiences.

Dataset

The dataset contains **200+ top streamed songs** with attributes like artist, streams, BPM, energy, danceability, and release year. Perfect for analyzing **genre trends**, **artist dominance**, and **audio feature correlations**.

Approach

I combined **Power Query** for cleaning, **DAX** for metrics, and **Power BI visuals** to narrate the music trends story.

Feature Correlation: Danceability & Popularity
Strong positive relationship

Why It Matters

This analysis gives **record labels and artists** data-backed insights into **what drives listener engagement** and helps them plan releases aligned with audience preferences.
