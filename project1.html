YouTube Trending & Engagement Analysis

A data story about what makes a video go viral

How It Started

I’ve always wondered: *What makes a YouTube video go viral?* To find out, I turned to the YouTube Trending Videos dataset on Kaggle — a fascinating collection of daily trending videos from multiple countries. It had everything from views and likes to tags and trending dates — but cleaning it was the real challenge.

The Challenges

Working with real-world data brought real-world problems: duplicates, missing descriptions, messy tags, and date formats that didn’t play well with time-series analysis. Combining multiple countries into one clean model required more than just basic filtering — it needed a structured approach.

The Cleaning & Modeling Journey

Here’s the process that turned chaos into clarity:

Power Query
Data import, merging, cleaning

DAX
Measures & calculated columns

Power BI Dashboard
Visual storytelling & insights

Using **Power Query**, I merged all country files, standardized dates, removed duplicates, and cleaned text fields. Then with **DAX**, I created custom metrics like engagement rate and dynamic rankings — turning raw data into meaningful KPIs for the dashboard.

What We Learned

Top Categories:

Peak Months:
May & June show viral surges
Best Times to Post:

Why It Matters

This dashboard doesn’t just show numbers — it tells a story. It reveals when and what to post, which categories perform best, and how creators can plan smarter strategies to grow their reach globally.
