Melbourne Housing Market Analysis

Exploring property trends & pricing dynamics with Power BI

Project Overview

The Melbourne real estate market is one of the most dynamic in Australia, influenced by location, property type, and market cycles. This project uses the Melbourne Housing Snapshot dataset to analyze property prices, suburbs, and attributes to uncover pricing trends and investment opportunities.

Dataset Details

The dataset contains over 34,000 property records including:

  • Property attributes: Price, type, rooms, land size, building area
  • Location: Suburb, postcode, region, council area
  • Market data: Sale date, distance from CBD, year built

This data is ideal for analyzing market hotspots, price distribution, and property size-to-price relationships.

Analytical Approach

I combined Power Query for data cleaning (handling missing values, standardizing suburbs) and DAX for KPIs such as median price by region, average property size, and price per square meter. The dashboard allows filtering by suburb, property type, and time period to explore market patterns interactively.
