Technical Approach

  • Utilized DAX functions to calculate Total Sales and Profit
  • Standardized all currencies to USD for consistency
  • Leveraged Power Query for comprehensive data cleaning and transformation

Customer Insights

  • 18K Total Customers with an Average Yearly Income of $57,306 across 6 countries
  • The 40-60 age group dominates the customer base (~11.8K customers)
  • 54.16% of customers are Single while 45.84% are Married
  • Commute Distance impacts customer distribution, with most residing within 0-1 miles (6.3K customers)

Sales & Profit Insights

  • Bikes represent the largest share of orders (60.45%), followed by Clothing (24.11%) and Accessories (15.44%)
  • The United States leads in total orders (1,741 orders), followed by Australia (1,150 orders)

Top Performing Products

  • Mountain-200 leads the revenue chart with $657K+, contributing to 302 total orders
  • Road-150 and Road-250 follow closely, with significant revenue and order volumes
