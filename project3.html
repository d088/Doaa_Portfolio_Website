Superstore Sales Analysis

Uncovering insights from Tableau’s Sample Superstore dataset

The Story Behind the Data

The **Sample Superstore** dataset is a classic example in the analytics world — rich with orders, customers, regions, and categories. My aim was to **turn this raw Excel file** from Tableau’s resources into a **visual story** that answers key business questions: *Which regions lead in sales? Which categories drive profits? When do sales peak?*

Preparing & Analyzing in Tableau

Starting with the Sample Superstore dataset, I explored trends in sales and profits across categories and states. Tableau’s built-in capabilities allowed me to clean minor inconsistencies, create calculated fields (profit ratios, average order value), and visualize data with **maps, line charts, and bar graphs**.
