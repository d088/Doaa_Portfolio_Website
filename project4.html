Google Play Store Cleaning & EDA

Uncovering trends from 10,000+ apps through Python EDA

The Challenge

The Google Play Store hosts millions of apps, but understanding what drives installs and ratings isn’t obvious. Using Kaggle’s Google Play Store dataset, I cleaned and explored over 10,000 app records — revealing key patterns hidden in messy, inconsistent data.

Cleaning & Exploration

The dataset had issues: missing ratings, inconsistent installs (e.g., “1,000+”), mixed formats, and irrelevant symbols in tags. Here’s how I tackled them using Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib):
