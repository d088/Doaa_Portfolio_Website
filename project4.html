Project Overview

Key Insights & Findings

  • Top Categories:
    • Family, Game, and Tools are the most common app categories.
  • Most Installed:
    • Apps in the Communication, Social, and Entertainment categories have the highest average number of installs.
  • Free Apps:
    • Over 90% of apps on the Play Store are free. Paid apps are mostly utilities or productivity tools.
  • Ratings:
    • Most apps are rated between 4.0 and 4.5, with very few rated below 3.0.
  • Correlation Analysis:
    • Number of reviews and installs are strongly positively correlated App size has no clear impact on app ratings
